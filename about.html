About



First Pointe is a blog devoted to discussing the areas of dance, with real, everyday dancers. Covering the topics of dance as a recreational activity, new styles, running businesses associated with dance and the acquisition of having dance in ones life.

Creator Sarah Paleologou, a dancer herself, has an intense passion for the art. This blog has allowed her to develop an insight upon a raft of inner corners surrounding dance.
